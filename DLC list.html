Changes the dlc have made to hardware requirements to run the game

Overlord: Added new mechanics like hyper relays, orbital rings, and vassal management, which increased CPU load in the mid-to-late game.

Nemesis: The introduction of increased espionage, AI calculations for new types of intelligence, and the "Become the Crisis" mechanics put further pressure on processor performance.

Apocalypse & Utopia: As the earliest major expansions, these added significantly more complexity to galactic management, resulting in the need for better hardware to handle massive empires and fleets compared to the 1.0 release.

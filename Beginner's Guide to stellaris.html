Empire selection

When first starting a game of Stellaris, you have the option to pick one of the preset empires to start with, or to make one of your own,
don't hit the random button if your new as its unpredictability can make the game harder to play.

leaving two options: preset empires and a custom-made one.

A preset empire allows you to go straight into the game without learning the overly complex empire creation screen.
But on the other hand a custom-made empire can be powerful since you would (hopefully)
understand it completely and be able to create a potentially powerful build. learning how to make one will come later in the guide.

Once you have then selected your empire and are ok with your world-gen options click the start button to load your first save.
when that has loaded keep the game paused as you learn what all the different buttons and menus do,
as well as learning how to move thought them and the galaxy map and system map
It is recommended that you explore the Stellaris Wiki as you explore Stellaris itself.
The knowledge its articles contain is used for the creation of this website have far more information than in here.
